In 3 Dimensions! but What is it? This Three.js sketch generates a 3D scene resembling a futuristic mech speeder. It creates a symmetrical composition of floating geometric shapes that form a structure reminiscent of a sci-fi vehicle or mecha. how does it work?
  • The sketch sets up a 3D scene with a floor and lighting.
  • It generates a number of boxes and bubbles with random properties.
  • Each box is mirrored across the vertical axis for symmetry.
  • An animation loop continuously updates and renders the scene.

The sketch includes orbit controls, allowing users to interact with the 3D view. By adjusting the main parameters, users can customize the appearance and complexity of the mech speeder structure.
